# Privacy Policy

**Offpath**
Last updated: May 8, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Offpath ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered travel planning app. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it. By using Offpath, you agree to this policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
When you create an account (including via Apple Sign-In), we collect:
- Your name (optional)
- Your email address
- A unique user identifier

### 2.2 Location Data
Offpath requests access to your device location **only while the app is in use** to show your position on the map. We do not track or store your location in the background.

### 2.3 Trip & Usage Data
- Destinations and itineraries you generate
- Places you save or interact with
- Trip memories and notes you add

### 2.4 Device Data
- Device type and operating system (for crash reporting and compatibility)
- App version

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Basis |
|---|---|
| Generate AI-powered trip itineraries | Contract (providing the service) |
| Show your location on the map | Consent (you grant location permission) |
| Sync your trips across devices | Contract |
| Improve the app | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with legal obligations | Legal obligation |

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** use your data for advertising.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Offpath uses the following third-party services which may process your data:

- **Google Places API** — for place search and map data (Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Groq** — for AI-generated itinerary and place content
- **Foursquare** — for place discovery data
- **Tavily** — for travel context and search
- **Render** — cloud hosting for our backend

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary with each provider.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account and trip data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Withdraw consent for location access at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **eneamuja07@gmail.com**.

**Account deletion** is available directly in the app under the You tab → Delete Account.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Offpath is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately.

---

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted storage (via Expo Secure Store) and HTTPS for all data in transit.
